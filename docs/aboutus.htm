About Us
As Bangalore's eminent builders and promoters, we understand people and their aspirations; their struggles and sacrifices; their pains and privations before they have a home of their own.

At Garden Grove properties, we spare no effort to ensure that the customer gets value for every rupee of his investment. This unique customer-centric approach to business has won us the trust and admiration of people across the country.
  • A team of high-caliber marketing, sales , architects, engineers, builders and planners.
  • An impressive array of residential complexes in and around Bangalore.
  • An enviable growth in terms of turnover, assets and customer-base.
  • And above all, an image as one of the most ethical, reliable and efficient organization.
  • Well, the past makes us proud and the future holds promise.
Garden Grove Properties will march ahead creating new landmarks in the cityscape and leaving a trail of satisfied customers.
Vision
To evolve into a 'No.1' construction company by setting new benchmarks for the industry with a right mix of talents, technology and ethical business practices.
Mission
To understand and fulfil customer's needs by delivering the most practical, high-quality building solutions at an affordable price and ensure they gain in every manner.
Garden Grove properties: a byword for quality
At Garden Grove properties, there is a rare and remarkable reverence for quality that encompasses all aspects of the organization-be it the raw material selection or the workforce or the process.
Thanks to the carefully nurtured Quality Policy, stringent quality checks are conducted at all levels - before, during and after construction. In fact, quality is a company wide effort to achieve customer satisfaction, productivity and profitability through product excellence.
